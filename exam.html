Chocolate Cake Recipe

This moist and delicious Chocolate cake is easy to make. It’s the perfect snack when you come home from school.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 40min
  • Preparation: 10min
  • Cooking: 30min

Ingredients

  • 100g caster sugar
  • 50g flour
  • 50g butter + nuts for the mould
  • 200g baking chocolate
  • 03 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (gas mark 6). In a saucepan, melt the chocolate and chopped butter over a very low heat.
  2. In a bowl, add the sugar, eggs and flour. Mix well.
  3. Add the chocolate/butter mixture. Mix well.
  4. Butter and flour your cake tin, then pour in the cake batter.
  5. Bake for about 20 minutes.
  6. When you take the cake out of the oven, it doesn't seem to be cooked enough. Let it cool, then unmould it.
